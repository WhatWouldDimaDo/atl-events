Scoring System

How Events Get Scored

Each event is scored 0–100 against a personal taste profile weighted toward electronic music, live experiences, and venue quality. Tier determines urgency of action.

S
Score 90–100
Buy tickets immediately, no debate. These are the reason to be in Atlanta.
Disclosure · Sub Focus 360°
A
Score 75–89
Strong interest — check lineup, coordinate group, act within 1–2 weeks.
DWTD + Magic Sword · Vintage Culture · Shaky Knees · Chet Faker
B
Score 60–74
Situational. Depends on who's free, energy levels, and competing options.
Jazz Fest · Streets Alive · Dino Fest
C
Score 0–59
Pass unless there's a strong social hook (friend visiting, birthday event, etc.)
Most pop shows · country · sports (unless ATL United)
Signal Amplifiers
Venue quality
The Eastern or Terminal West adds 5–10 points automatically. Sound quality is non-negotiable.
Format rarity
Live band vs. DJ set, 360° immersive vs. standard stage — format uniqueness gets weighted heavily.
Social alignment
The right show with the right group elevates every tier. Invite matrix is baked into action plans.
